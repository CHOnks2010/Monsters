Bosses

Every boss drops health, armor and/or ammo when significantly damaged and/or killed.

Cyberdemon

Attacks:

  • Missiles attached to its back and arm cannon

  • Back missiles land in random spots shown in hell circles

  • Has a laser arm that acts like the Hell Razer arm but deals exponentially more damage

  • A sword that releases a wave of fire

Abilities:

  • Summons walls randomly in the second phase of the fight

Movement:

  • Stays in one spot but sometimes dashes across the area of combat

How to beat:

  • Keep your distance and lower his health with long-range weapons

  • Once staggered, go in for the melee attack

  • This will trigger the second phase where you will be in hell

  • Do the same thing and use the BFG-9000 to win the fight

Hell Guards

1. The Father Hell Guard

Attacks:

  • Has 2 wielding hammers

  • Will ram head first into you

  • Waves of fireballs

  • Spins creating fire waves

  • Creates a yellow electric orb that releases Argent rounds

Defense:

  • Randomly has a green orb surrounding itself negating all damage done to it

How to beat:

  • Have a short enough distance where you can use the Super Shotgun but avoid the hammers

  • When the green defense is up, just dodge all of its attacks and wait until it disappears

  • Jump over the fire when necessary

  • Move out of the way just before the ram hits you

  • When the Yellow Orb shows, immediately rush in and shoot using the Super Shotgun as it can stun the Hell Guard

2. The Twin Hell Guards

Attacks:

  • There are 2 separate Hell Guards who have 2 separate attacks with no defense

  • One has the Yellow Orb while the other has the 2 wielding hammers and fire attacks

Abilities:

  • Both can dash

How to beat:

  • Focus on the twin that has the Yellow Orb ability as you can stun and do insane amounts of damage using the Super Shotgun

  • Note that they both share the same health bar so they both share the damage dealt to one twin

  • Once the Yellow Orb twin is staggered, kill

  • The second twin's health will already be very low and can easily be killed using any long-range and shotgun

Spider Mastermind

Attacks:

  • Laser beams

  • Laser gun

  • Missiles

  • Arm/leg that attacks the area of combat

  • Releasing little orbs that explode on contact

  • Can electrify the whole ground

  • Creates spikes on the pillars

  • Can sometimes throw explosive items

How to beat:

  • Any attack you make should be targeted at the exposed brain

  • Keep your distance as much as possible

  • Use long-range weapons

  • When the laser beams attack, focus on the one at the front: jump over the first, duck the second, and jump the third

  • When she electrifies the ground, double jump onto the pillars and keep firing at the brain

  • When she is close to stopping the electricity, jump off the pillars

  • Keep repeating this until staggered, then kill

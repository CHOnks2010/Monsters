Bosses

 

Every boss drops health, armor, and/or ammo when significantly damaged or killed.

 

Cyberdemon

 

Attacks:

 
       
  • Missiles from back and arm cannon
    •    
  • Laser arm with high damage
    •    
  • Fire wave sword
    •  
 

Abilities:

 
       
  • Summons walls in second phase
    •  
 

Movement:

 
       
  • Mostly stationary, occasional dashes
    •  
 

How to beat:

 
       
  • Use long-range weapons to lower health
    •    
  • Melee when staggered to trigger phase two
    •    
  • Repeat and use BFG-9000 to finish
    •  
 

Hell Guards

 

1. Father Hell Guard

 

Attacks:

 
       
  • Dual hammers, head rams, fireball waves
    •    
  • Spins with fire waves
    •    
  • Yellow electric orb with Argent rounds
    •  
 

Defense:

 
       
  • Green orb negates all damage
    •  
 

How to beat:

 
       
  • Use Super Shotgun at close range
    •    
  • Dodge attacks while shield is active
    •    
  • Rush and shoot when yellow orb appears
    •  
 

2. Twin Hell Guards

 

Attacks:

 
       
  • One has yellow orb, other has hammers and fire
    •  
 

Abilities:

 
       
  • Both can dash
    •  
 

How to beat:

 
       
  • Focus on yellow orb twin for stun damage
    •    
  • Shared health bar — damage one to weaken both
    •    
  • Finish second twin with long-range or shotgun
    •  
 

Spider Mastermind

 

Attacks:

 
       
  • Laser beams, laser gun, missiles
    •    
  • Arm/leg area attacks
    •    
  • Exploding orbs, ground electricity
    •    
  • Pillar spikes, thrown explosives
    •  
 

How to beat:

 
       
  • Target exposed brain
    •    
  • Keep distance and use long-range weapons
    •    
  • Jump/dodge laser patterns
    •    
  • Use pillars to avoid electricity
    •    
  • Repeat until staggered, then kill
    •

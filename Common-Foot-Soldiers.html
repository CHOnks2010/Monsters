Common Foot Soldiers

 

Imp

 
       
  • Common demons found all over the game in every mission
    •    
  • Climbs on walls and hangs from any and all surfaces
    •    
  • Throws fireballs
    •    
  • Very easy to kill
    •  
 

Unwilling

 
       
  • They just slap you
    •    
  • Easy to kill
    •    
  • Useful for ammo farming and trickshots
    •  
 

Possessed

 
       
  • Copies the behaviours of Unwilling in combat
    •    
  • Easy to Kill
    •  
 

Possessed Engineer

 
       
  • Has a fuel tank attached to the body
    •    
  • Will kill itself by charging into you and exploding
    •    
  • Shoot the fuel tank before confrontation or kill from a distance
    •    
  • Can kill with a Chainsaw
    •  
 

Hell Razer

 
       
  • Has a laser beam that shoots in a straight line and does progressive damage
    •    
  • Takes one super shotgun shot to kill or stagger
    •    
  • Go in close range to avoid sniper shots
    •  
 

Possessed Security

 
       
  • Has a shield that can tank multiple shots
    •    
  • Shield turns purple then red before deactivating
    •    
  • Shield reactivates quickly — attack from behind with Super Shotgun
    •    
  • Staggered state disables shield
    •
